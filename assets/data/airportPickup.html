Airport Pickup

Pre-requisites

Before you request an airport pickup from a Grad Student, please ensure that you meet all the below conditions:-

  • Received an admit and I-20 from UNC-Chapel Hill
  • Confirmed F-1 Visa
  • Confirmed Airline Ticket

Airport Pickup Request

Around mid July, shoot an email to "unc_desi (at) googlegroups.com" requesting access to the Airport Pickup Google Doc where you can fill in your Name, UNC PID, Arrival Date, Email, Contact No. and Flight details. After you make an entry, a grad student will be assigned to you who will be responsible for your airport pickup. The contact details of him/her will be emailed to you so that you can get in touch.

What if no one comes to pick me up?

You will have the contact no. of the person, wait for around 45-60 mins, if he/she doesn't turn up, call them. Mostly, someone or the other will come to pick you up. In the worst case, if you know where you are going to be temporarily housed, go to the Ground Transportation area in the airport and pick up an RDU Taxi, it costs $30 (fixed rate) from airport to Chapel Hill. Please confirm the fixed rate before you get into the taxi and don't forget to tip 10% once you get down.
