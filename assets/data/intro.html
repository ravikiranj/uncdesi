Introduction

Welcome to UNC New Indian Grad Student Unofficial Guide. This guide is the one shop stop for all your basic doubts/queries related to being a UNC student. In this guide, we have tried covering a lot of basic info such as packing, pre-arrival, post-arrival, campus life, housing, finance etc.

Although this guide is exhaustive, there will always be something that didn't strike us and in such a case, please feel free to contact us at "unc_desi (at) googlegroups.com".
