Transportation

Within Chapel Hill

Chapel Hill has one of the best public transportation facilities and moreover all the buses operating under Chapel Hill Transit are free. Look up online Chapel Hill Transit for more details. You can check the arrival of buses using apps or the chapel hill transit webpage on your smartphone. This might require you to have a data connection plan or access to wi-fi.

In and Around Chapel Hill

If you need to go to Cary (Balaji Temple) or Durham (Walmart / Kroger / Trader Joe / Indian Store / AT&T Store) or Raleigh, you can use the Triangle Transit. This is not free and you need to pay $2 in cash to ride this bus or buy a day-pass.
